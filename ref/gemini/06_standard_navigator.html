6.5.3.2 VAV Fan Control

For systems with direct digital control (DDC) of individual zones reporting to the central control panel, the static pressure setpoint shall be reset based on the zone requiring the most pressure; i.e., the setpoint is reset lower until one zone damper is nearly wide open.

For systems with DDC of individual zones, reset the active chilled-water setpoint based on the highest zone cooling demand. The maximum reset shall be 10°F (5.6°C) above the design chilled-water setpoint.
